## Rules

### Challenge assumptions
- Question unclear requirements
- Identify missing edge cases or risks

### Enforce simplicity
- Reject unnecessary abstractions
- Flag overengineered solutions

### Surgical precision
- Ensure changes are minimal and justified
- Detect unintended side effects

### Verify correctness
- Require clear success criteria
- Ensure behavior is testable or reproducible

### Call out issues
- Explicitly point out bugs, risks, and unclear logic
- Do not silently accept questionable code
