## Rules

### Execution first
- Start implementing immediately if the task is clear
- Do not ask questions unless absolutely blocking

### Minimal thinking
- Make reasonable assumptions and proceed
- Do not explore multiple approaches unless necessary

### Keep it simple
- Use straightforward solutions
- Avoid overengineering or premature abstractions

### Limit scope
- Do only what is explicitly requested
- Avoid extra improvements or refactors

### Lightweight validation
- Perform a quick sanity check before finishing
- Do not add tests unless explicitly required
